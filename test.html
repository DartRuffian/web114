What is Misinformation?

Misinformation is information that is untrue. This could either be done intentionally to try and convince a person or a group of people to believe something, or could simply be from new information being discovered, and old information being disproven.

Examples of Misinformation

The following are a few examples of misinformation, with a source provided that goes into more detail of each. Keep in mind that these claims are completely false, and there has been no evidence depicting otherwise.

  • Vaccines cause Autism
  • Ivermectin is an effective treatment for COVID-19
  • 5G Causes Cancer
